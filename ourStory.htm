Throwback to 1992!

Couple Craig and Wendy Anderson were out on a hike in Discovery Trail in Seattle Washington when they had realized all the ice in their cooler had melted, leaving them with soggy sandwiches and soaked drinks. Coolers were not up to standard outdoor conditions in this time & they decided that needed to change.

In 1992 we founded ZeroX and our mission was to create a cooler than is suitable for work and play. You can bring it to the tailgate, hunting range or camping site and keep all your drinks and snacks fresh for hours on end!

We are a customer oriented company and strive to keep improving our products and ourselves to help fit our customers needs, never failing to put the “cool” back into “cooler”
